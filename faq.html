üîë Rentals & Experiences

To rent from EM Luxury Cars, you must:
  • Be at least 25 years old (30+ for hypercars)
  • Hold a valid driver's license for 3+ years
  • Provide proof of full-coverage insurance ($500K+ liability)
  • Present a valid credit card for security deposit ($5,000-$50,000)
  • Pass a basic driving record check

Yes, our standard rentals include 100 miles per day. Additional options:
  • Extra Miles: $3-$10 per mile (varies by vehicle)
  • Unlimited Package: Available for select vehicles
  • Track Day Events: No mileage restrictions on our track

Our cancellation policy is flexible:
  • 7+ days before: Full refund
  • 3-6 days before: 50% refund
  • Less than 3 days: No refund, credit for future rental
  • No-show: Full charge applies
